NP Membership (Non-Profit Organizations) - Free

  • Networking Opportunities: Access to networking events with business leaders and other non-profits.
  • Promotion: Free listing in the Chamber’s online directory and event promotions.
  • Educational Resources: Access to workshops, seminars, and webinars tailored for non-profits.
  • Advocacy: Representation on key issues affecting non-profit organizations at the local and state levels.

Bronze Membership - $150/month

  • Business Listings: Inclusion in the Chamber’s online and printed business directories.
  • Networking Events: Invitations to member-only networking events and mixers.
  • Discounts: Access to member-to-member discounts and special rates for Chamber events.
  • Marketing Opportunities: Ability to post promotions and events on the Chamber’s website and social media channels.

Silver Membership -$300/month

  • Enhanced Listings: Highlighted business listing in the Chamber’s directories.
  • Increased Visibility: Opportunities to host and sponsor events for greater exposure.
  • Educational Opportunities: Access to advanced workshops, training sessions, and business development seminars.
  • Marketing and Advertising: Discounted advertising rates in Chamber publications and on the website.

Gold Membership - $1000/month

  • Premier Listings: Top-tier business listing with premium placement in directories.
  • Exclusive Events: Invitations to VIP events and private receptions with community leaders.
  • Leadership Opportunities: Eligibility for Chamber board positions and committees.
  • Comprehensive Marketing: Extensive advertising opportunities, including featured spots in newsletters and social media highlights.
