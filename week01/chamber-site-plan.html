Overview

Site Name: Calgary Chamber of Commerce

This name represent the city where companies operate to look for their interests

Purpose

The Calgary Chamber of commerce seeks to provide a setting for companies to network and exchange ideas that can help improve Calgary's industry and mantain it as a referent in Canada

Also, it helps to group the industry to discuss current issues that might affect the industry and how to give back to the community

Scenarios

  1. What requirements are needed to join the chamber?
  2. What is the procedure to join the chamber?
  3. What is the price of membership?
